## The shift

Markets reward teams that orchestrate the **full lifecycle**—onboarding, success milestones, renewals, and expansion—in one coherent story.

## Practical move

Rebalance instrumentation and incentives so post-sale signals influence the same planning rhythms as top-of-funnel spend.
